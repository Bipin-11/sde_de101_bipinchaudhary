<h1>Assignment</h1>

<p>Imagine that you are hired to enable data analytics; where would you start? How would you go about gathering requirements? Hint: Think backward from what the business may need.</p>

Define goals: Understand business objectives and KPIs.

Gather requirements: Interview stakeholders to determine their data needs and desired insights.

Assess Current Data: Inventory existing data sources and evaluate data quality.

Specify Data Needs: Identify necessary data types, sources, and transformations.

Plan infrastructure: Choose tools for data storage, processing, and visualization; design the architecture.

Implement and test: Build and test data solutions; validate with users.

Deploy and monitor: Roll out solutions, monitor performance, and optimize as needed.
